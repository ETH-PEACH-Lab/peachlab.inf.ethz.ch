## Our Research Approach 

**Epistemic Stance:** Our research is grounded in human-computer interaction, informed by interaction design, cognitive science, and learning sciences. 
From our base in computer science, we bring an engineering mindset to interdisciplinary problems, committing to both **empirical and constructive problems**. 
We study real-world use of technology to describe and explain human behavior, while also building interactive systems to explore ideas. 
In constructive work, we do not emphasize on engineering fully polished systems for deployment, but on generating understanding through prototyping and empirical evaluation. We treat system-building as a means of inquiry where we use artifacts to surface design principles, learning mechanisms, and interaction patterns.

In short, while some may see prototyping as just building toys, we embrace that — our toys are how we test ideas and generate new knowledge.
