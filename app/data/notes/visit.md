## Getting to ETH Zurich

### From Zurich Airport (ZRH)

<div style="display:flex; gap:24px; align-items:flex-start; flex-wrap:wrap;">
<div style="flex:1; min-width:280px;">

Zurich Airport is extremely well connected, and the train station is located directly beneath the terminal—just follow signs for **“Bahn/Rail.”**

**Train → Zürich HB (Main Station)**
- Duration: ~10 minutes  
- Frequency: Every 5–10 minutes  
- Cost: ~7 CHF (2nd class)

Tickets can be purchased at SBB machines or via the [SBB Mobile App](https://www.sbb.ch/en). Contactless payment is supported everywhere.

### From Zürich HB to ETH

<div style="display:flex; gap:24px; align-items:flex-start; flex-wrap:wrap;">
<div style="flex:1; min-width:280px;">

ETH’s main campus (Zentrum) sits on a hill just above the city center. From Zürich HB:

- **Polybahn** — The easiest and most iconic option (2 minutes, included in transit ticket)  
- **Walk** — ~10–15 minutes uphill through the old town  
- **Tram** — Lines 6, 9, or 10 → *ETH/Universitätsspital*

💡 *Tip:* If it’s your first visit, take the Polybahn. It drops you right at the main building.

## ETH Zurich Campus

<div style="display:flex; gap:24px; align-items:flex-start; flex-wrap:wrap;">
<div style="flex:1; min-width:280px;">

ETH Zurich is consistently ranked among the world’s leading universities in science and technology. The Zentrum campus is compact, walkable, and offers some of the best views in the city.

**Places to explore:**
- [Main Building (HG)](https://ethz.ch/en/campus/access/zentrum.html)
- [Self-Guided Campus Tour](https://ethz.ch/en/campus/getting-to-know/discover/visit-by-yourself/flyer.html), don't forget to check out the famous Einstein locker
- [ML Hall](https://ethz.ch/staffnet/en/news-and-events/internal-news/archive/2025/11/robots-move-into-ml-hall.html)
- [Student Project House (SPH)](https://sph.ethz.ch/)  
- [HCI Community](https://hci.ethz.ch/)

💡 *Don’t miss:* The terrace behind the main building. On a clear day, you can see the Alps.

## Explore Zurich

Zurich is compact, safe, and easy to explore. Most places are within 15–20 minutes.

**Nature & Outdoors**
- [Lake Zurich](https://www.zuerich.com/en/visit/nature/lake-zurich): Walking, swimming, boat rides  
- [Uetliberg](https://www.zuerich.com/en/visit/nature/uetliberg): Local mountain with panoramic views  
- [Hiking](https://www.zuerich.com/en/sightseeing-activities/sport-and-relaxation/hiking): Quick access to nature  

**Culture & History**
- [Old Town (Altstadt)](https://www.myswitzerland.com/en-ch/experiences/zurichs-old-town/): Historic center  
- [Kunsthaus Zürich](https://www.zuerich.com/de/besuchen/kultur/kunsthaus-zuerich): Major art museum  
- [Grossmünster](https://www.zuerich.com/en/visit/attractions/grossmunster): Landmark church  

**Food & Shopping**
- [Bahnhofstrasse](https://www.zuerich.com/de/sightseeing-aktivitaeten/shopping/bahnhofstrasse)  
- [Lindt Home of Chocolate](https://www.zuerich.com/de/besuchen/kultur/lindt-home-of-chocolate)


## Best Times to Visit

- **Spring** — [Cherry blossoms](https://www.zuerich.com/en/inform-plan/seasons-and-climate/zurich-in-spring/cherry-blossoms-in-zurich), [rose fountains](https://swissfamilyfun.com/zurich-rose-fountains/)
- **Summer** — [River swimming](https://www.zuerich.com/en/sightseeing-activities/sport-and-relaxation/water-sports/river-swimming), lake swimming
- **Fall** — [Great hiking](https://www.zuerich.com/en/inform-plan/seasons-and-climate/zurich-in-the-fall)
- **Winter** — [Christmas markets](https://www.zuerich.com/en/sightseeing-activities/shopping/christmas-markets), cozy atmosphere


## Practical Tips

- **Public transport**: Extremely reliable; use the [SBB app](https://www.sbb.ch/en)  
- **Language**: German locally, English widely spoken  
- **Currency**: CHF; cards accepted almost everywhere  
- **Weather**: Can change quickly → bring layers  
- **Tipping**: Not required
